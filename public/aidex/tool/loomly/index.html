About Loomly

Loomly is a brand management platform that helps teams create, schedule, and optimize social media content. It provides post ideas based on trending topics, date-based events, and RSS feeds. Loomly's optimization tips help improve each post before publishing, and the approval workflow ensures brand consistency.
