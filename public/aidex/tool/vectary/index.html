About Vectary

Vectary is an online 3D and AR design platform that makes professional 3D content accessible through a browser-based editor. It includes AI-powered features for material generation, scene composition, and product visualization. The platform supports creating interactive 3D product configurators, AR try-on experiences, and embeddable 3D viewers for e-commerce. Used by product designers, marketers, and e-commerce brands.

Key Features

  • Browser-based 3D editor
  • AI material generation
  • Product configurators
  • AR experience creation
  • Embeddable 3D viewers
  • 3D model library
  • Photorealistic rendering
  • Collaboration tools

Pros

  • No download needed
  • Great for product visualization
  • AR experience creation
  • Embeddable 3D viewers
  • Good for e-commerce use

Cons

  • Limited animation features
  • Performance limited for large scenes
