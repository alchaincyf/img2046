About Semrush (Research Features)

Semrush offers over 55 products, tools and add-ons across online visibility management, with data for more than 142 countries. The Traffic & Market Toolkit provides detailed traffic analysis, audience insights, competitive benchmarking, and market trend identification. It includes traffic breakdown across all channels (Organic, Paid, Direct, Social, Referral, and AI Traffic), a Growth Quadrant tool to visually map competitors, and access to extensive historical data. The .Trends suite extends core functionality with market-level competitive intelligence including market explorer, traffic analytics, and EyeOn competitor monitoring.

Key Features

  • Traffic & Market Toolkit for competitive benchmarking
  • Growth Quadrant competitor mapping
  • Multi-channel traffic breakdown including AI traffic
  • Historical trend data across years
