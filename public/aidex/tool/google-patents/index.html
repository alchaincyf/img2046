About Google Patents

Google Patents is a free patent search engine that draws on patents from more than 100 of the biggest patent offices around the world, enabling searches across more than 120 million documents. Full-text patent documents are indexed from 20 of the biggest patent offices. The platform offers simple and advanced search with multiple fields including date, inventor, assignee, patent office, languages, status, and patent type. Search results integrate materials from Google Scholar, Google Books, and Google's Prior Art Archive. Users can set up alerts for specific search terms or patent applications.

Key Features

  • Full-text search across 120M+ patent documents
  • Coverage from 100+ global patent offices
  • Advanced search with boolean operators
  • Patent alerts for monitoring
  • Integration with Google Scholar and Google Books
  • Prior Art Archive integration
  • No registration required

Pros

  • Completely free with no usage limits
  • Massive database of 120M+ documents
  • No login or registration required
  • Intuitive Google-style search interface
  • Good for initial patent landscape exploration

Cons

  • No AI-powered analytics or insights
  • Limited filtering compared to professional tools
  • No patent landscape visualization
  • Cannot replace professional patent analysis tools
  • No collaboration or project management features
