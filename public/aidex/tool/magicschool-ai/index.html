About MagicSchool AI

MagicSchool AI is built specifically for educators, offering over 60 AI-powered tools for lesson planning, content creation, differentiation, and communication. Teachers can generate lesson plans tied to grade-level standards, adapt texts for different reading levels, create assessments, and draft parent communications. Used by thousands of schools worldwide.
