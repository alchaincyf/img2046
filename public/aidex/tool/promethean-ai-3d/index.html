About Promethean AI

Promethean AI automates virtual world building and 3D environment design using natural language commands. Artists give semantic commands like 'Make a bedroom' and the AI intelligently places appropriate assets. It manages 3D assets visually and semantically without strict naming conventions. Integrates with Unreal Engine, Unity, 3ds Max, Maya, and Blender. Claims up to 10x increase in production speed for environment dressing.

Key Features

  • Natural language scene generation
  • Intelligent asset placement
  • Semantic asset understanding
  • Unreal, Unity, Maya, Blender support
