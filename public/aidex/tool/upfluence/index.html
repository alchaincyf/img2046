About Upfluence

Upfluence is an influencer marketing platform that uses AI to match brands with the most relevant creators. It features influencer search with detailed filters, campaign management, content approval workflows, and affiliate program management. The platform integrates with major e-commerce platforms for product seeding and sales tracking.
