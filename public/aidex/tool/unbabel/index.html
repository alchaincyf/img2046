About Unbabel

Unbabel provides AI-powered translation specifically designed for customer experience, enabling support teams to communicate with customers in their native language. The platform combines LLM-based translation with human quality review to deliver fast, accurate translations for customer service interactions. Unbabel integrates with Zendesk, Salesforce, Intercom, and other CX platforms.

Key Features

  • LLM-based customer support translation
  • Human quality assurance layer
  • Zendesk, Salesforce, Intercom integrations
  • Real-time chat and email translation
  • Quality scoring and monitoring
  • Custom glossary and style guides
  • Analytics and reporting
  • Multi-channel support

Pros

  • Purpose-built for customer experience
  • Strong CX platform integrations
  • Combines AI speed with human quality
  • Pioneered COMET quality metric

Cons

  • Enterprise-only, not for individuals
  • Focus limited to customer support use cases
  • Pricing not transparent
