About Noom

Noom is a weight management app that uses AI and cognitive behavioral therapy (CBT) to help users develop healthier habits. The platform provides personalized meal plans, food logging with a color-coded system, daily lessons on psychology-based behavior change, and access to health coaches. Noom's AI adapts to each user's progress and preferences, making it one of the most popular digital health programs for sustainable weight loss.

Key Features

  • AI-personalized weight loss program
  • CBT-based behavior change lessons
  • Food logging with color-coded system
  • Personalized meal plans
  • Health coach access
  • Exercise tracking integration
  • Progress analytics and insights
  • Community support groups

Pros

  • Psychology-based approach to lasting change
  • Personalized AI-driven coaching
  • Comprehensive food database
  • Focus on behavior change, not just calories

Cons

  • Relatively expensive subscription
  • Can be overwhelming with daily lessons
  • Coach quality varies
