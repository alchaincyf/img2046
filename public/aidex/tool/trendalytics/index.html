About Trendalytics

Trendalytics uses AI to analyze consumer search data, social media trends, and market signals to provide fashion trend intelligence. The platform helps brands and retailers identify emerging trends, optimize buying decisions, and create data-driven product strategies. It combines Google search data with social signals for demand forecasting.

Key Features

  • Search trend analysis
  • Social trend detection
  • Demand forecasting
  • Competitive benchmarking
  • Assortment planning
  • Product development insights
  • Market sizing
