About Lever

Lever combines applicant tracking and CRM capabilities in a single platform, enabling recruiting teams to source, nurture, and manage candidates throughout the hiring lifecycle. Its AI-powered features include candidate matching, automated outreach, and advanced analytics.

Key Features

  • Combined ATS + CRM
  • AI candidate matching
  • Automated nurture campaigns
  • Visual pipeline management
  • Advanced analytics
  • EEO/OFCCP compliance
  • Referral management

Pros

  • Unified ATS and CRM
  • Clean intuitive interface
  • Strong nurture capabilities
  • Good diversity features

Cons

  • Limited customization options
  • No public pricing
  • Reporting could be deeper
