About Google Travel

Google Travel is Google's comprehensive travel planning hub that brings together flights, hotels, vacation rentals, and things to do in one place. The platform leverages Google's AI infrastructure to provide price insights, destination recommendations, and trip planning tools. Google is actively developing AI Mode Canvas for visual travel planning, which integrates flight and hotel data with interactive trip boards. Google Travel's strength lies in its integration with the broader Google ecosystem including Maps, Search, and Calendar.

Key Features

  • Unified flights, hotels, and activities search
  • AI Mode Canvas for visual trip planning
  • Price tracking and alerts
  • Destination exploration tools
  • Integration with Google Maps and Calendar
  • Saved trips and places
  • Budget-based destination discovery

Pros

  • Seamless Google ecosystem integration
  • Completely free with no hidden costs
  • AI Mode Canvas is innovative for visual planning
  • Massive data coverage

Cons

  • Doesn't complete bookings (redirects to providers)
  • AI features still rolling out globally
  • Can be overwhelming with options
