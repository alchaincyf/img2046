About Consensus

Consensus is an AI-powered academic search engine that answers questions with research-backed evidence from peer-reviewed scientific papers. Rather than returning lists of papers, Consensus synthesizes findings from multiple studies to provide direct, evidence-based answers with citation support. The platform is designed for researchers, students, health professionals, and anyone who needs answers grounded in scientific literature rather than web content. Consensus analyzes the conclusions and findings of papers to determine the strength of evidence for or against a claim.

Key Features

  • Evidence-based answers from scientific papers
  • Consensus meter showing research agreement
  • Citation export in multiple formats
  • Study quality indicators
  • Topic synthesis across multiple papers
  • Claim analysis with evidence strength
  • Filter by study type and date
  • Bookmarks and reading lists

Pros

  • Unique evidence synthesis from real papers
  • Consensus meter shows scientific agreement
  • Trustworthy, citation-backed answers
  • Great for evidence-based decision making
  • Clean, focused interface

Cons

  • Limited to academic/scientific content
  • Smaller paper database than Google Scholar
  • Not useful for non-research queries
  • Advanced features require subscription
