About Canva AI Image Generator

Canva's Magic Media is a powerful AI image and video generation tool integrated into the world's most popular online design platform. It uses text-to-image and text-to-video capabilities to create custom visuals from scratch, making professional design accessible to everyone. With Canva Pro, users get approximately 500 AI uses per month, plus access to the broader design ecosystem including templates, stock photos, and collaboration features. The seamless integration makes it ideal for creating social media graphics, presentations, marketing materials, and more.

Key Features

  • 500 AI uses/month (Pro plan)
