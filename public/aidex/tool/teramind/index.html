About Teramind

Teramind is an insider risk management and user activity monitoring (UAM) platform combining DLP, productivity analytics, and behavioral forensics in a single solution. The platform continuously monitors employee and contractor activity across endpoints, capturing keystrokes, screenshots, website visits, application usage, emails, and file transfers to detect data leakage, fraud, and policy violations. Teramind's behavior-based rules engine can automatically block, alert, or lock sessions in real time. It's widely used in financial services, healthcare, and government for compliance programs and insider threat management.

Key Features

  • Real-time user activity monitoring (keystrokes, screenshots, web, apps)
  • DLP with content inspection for files, email, and cloud uploads
  • Behavior-based rule engine with automatic blocking and session alerts
  • OCR for screenshot content analysis to catch image-based data leakage
  • Remote monitoring with covert and transparent deployment modes
  • Forensic playback of recorded user sessions
  • Productivity analytics with idle time and focus measurement reports
  • Integration with SIEM, HR, and ticketing systems

Pros

  • Transparent pricing with affordable starting plans at $13/user/month
  • Comprehensive activity monitoring including OCR and session recording
  • Flexible deployment for cloud, on-premises, and hybrid environments
  • Dual-mode monitoring (visible to employees or covert for investigations)

Cons

  • Extensive monitoring may raise employee privacy concerns in some regions
  • Requires careful policy design to avoid over-monitoring
  • Performance impact on monitored endpoints with full recording enabled
  • Complex configuration for fine-grained DLP rules and thresholds
