About Browzwear

Browzwear is an enterprise 3D fashion design platform used by major brands for virtual product creation. It creates digital twins of garments with accurate fabric behavior, enables virtual fitting rooms, and supports the entire product development lifecycle from design to production. The platform integrates with PLM systems and other fashion tech tools.

Key Features

  • 3D garment design
  • Digital twin technology
  • Virtual fitting room
  • Fabric simulation
  • PLM integration
  • Collaboration tools
  • Production-ready output
