About ProcessMaker

ProcessMaker combines workflow automation, process modeling, and process mining in an open-core platform, suitable for organizations seeking flexible workflow and mining capabilities. The platform allows both business users and developers to design, automate, and optimize business processes through a visual interface. ProcessMaker can augment or replace Celonis use cases, with customers using Celonis for SAP-focused core process mining while leveraging ProcessMaker speed and flexibility for finance or procurement process analysis across different business applications. The platform supports low-code development, making it accessible to citizen developers while providing API and code-level customization for technical teams. ProcessMaker is ideal for mid-market companies that need robust BPM without enterprise complexity.
