About Kling AI

Kling AI by Kuaishou is a powerful AI video generation platform that game developers use for creating cinematics, trailers, promotional videos, and in-game cutscenes. It generates high-quality video content from text prompts and images, supporting game developers who need cinematic content without traditional video production. The motion quality and consistency make it suitable for game marketing and story-driven cutscene prototyping.

Key Features

  • AI video generation from text
  • Image-to-video creation
  • High-quality motion generation
  • Multiple resolution options
  • Style control and guidance
  • Batch video generation
  • Commercial use licensing
  • Fast generation speed
