About Landbot

Landbot is a no-code conversational AI platform for building chatbots across WhatsApp, web, and messaging channels. Its drag-and-drop builder makes it easy to create complex conversation flows with AI capabilities, lead qualification, and integration with CRM and marketing tools.

Key Features

  • Drag-and-drop builder
  • WhatsApp integration
  • AI responses
  • Lead qualification
  • CRM integration
  • Conditional logic
  • Analytics

Pros

  • Intuitive visual builder
  • Strong WhatsApp support
  • Good for lead gen
  • No coding needed

Cons

  • Limited AI compared to dedicated platforms
  • Can get expensive
  • WhatsApp requires Business API
