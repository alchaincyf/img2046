About Notion for Design

Notion is widely adopted by design teams as their primary workspace for design documentation, component specs, brand guidelines, user research, and project management. Design teams use Notion to document design systems, maintain brand guidelines, track design requests, and conduct design reviews. Notion AI adds capabilities for writing first drafts of content, summarizing research, and auto-filling databases.

Key Features

  • Flexible wiki-style documentation
  • Database views for design requests
  • AI writing and summarization
  • Rich media embedding (Figma, videos)
  • Team collaboration and comments
  • Templates for design processes

Pros

  • Extremely flexible for any design documentation need
  • Strong AI features for content creation
  • Good free plan
  • Widely adopted makes collaboration easy

Cons

  • Not a dedicated design tool
  • Can be overwhelming with setup
  • Performance can lag with large databases
  • Not specialized for design system documentation
