About Typeform

Typeform is a conversational form and survey platform that presents questions one at a time in a visually clean interface, resulting in significantly higher completion rates than traditional forms. With AI-powered form generation, users can create complete surveys from a simple description. Typeform is used for lead generation, customer feedback, quizzes, and research surveys.

Key Features

  • AI form and survey generation
  • Conversational one-question interface
  • Advanced logic jumps
  • Calculator and quiz scoring
  • Payment collection via Stripe
  • Integration with 300+ apps
  • Analytics and completion tracking
  • Custom branding and themes

Pros

  • Beautiful, engaging form experience
  • High completion rates vs traditional forms
  • AI generation from description
  • Rich logic and branching
  • Payment collection built in

Cons

  • Expensive for high response volumes
  • Free tier very limited
  • Less suitable for long surveys
  • Some advanced features require Business
