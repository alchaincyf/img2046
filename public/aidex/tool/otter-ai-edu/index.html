About Otter.ai for Education

Otter.ai provides real-time AI transcription for lectures, meetings, and study sessions. Students can record lectures and get instant, searchable transcripts with AI-generated summaries. The education plan offers special features for students including automatic slide capture, action items, and collaborative note-sharing.

Key Features
