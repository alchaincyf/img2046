About Iconosquare

Iconosquare specializes in deep social media analytics for Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, LinkedIn, and X. It provides industry benchmarking, competitor tracking, hashtag monitoring, and detailed engagement metrics. The platform also includes scheduling, conversation management, and reporting features for agencies.
