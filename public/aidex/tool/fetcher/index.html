About Fetcher

Fetcher combines AI technology with human expertise to source and deliver qualified candidate profiles to recruiters. It automates the tedious parts of sourcing — finding, vetting, and reaching out to passive candidates — so recruiters can focus on relationship building.

Key Features

  • AI-powered candidate sourcing
  • Automated email outreach
  • Candidate diversity filters
  • Calendar scheduling
  • Analytics dashboard
  • Team collaboration
  • ATS integrations

Pros

  • Automates sourcing heavily
  • Good candidate quality
  • Easy to set up
  • Saves recruiter hours

Cons

  • Limited to sourcing use case
  • Pricing per user adds up
  • Less control over search criteria
