About Matterport

Matterport is the leading platform for creating digital twins of physical spaces—buildings, homes, construction sites, retail stores, and industrial facilities. Using Matterport's Pro3 or Pro2 cameras, or compatible 3rd-party scanners, users capture photorealistic 3D scans that are processed by Matterport's cloud AI into immersive virtual tours, floor plans, measurement data, and 3D mesh exports. The platform is used in real estate, construction (progress documentation), insurance (damage assessment), and retail (virtual stores). Matterport's AI automatically generates accurate floor plans, room measurements, and BIM-compatible models from scans.

Key Features

  • Immersive 3D virtual tours with Dollhouse and Floor Plan views
  • AI-generated accurate floor plans with room measurements
  • Digital twin creation for construction and facility management
  • BIM-compatible model export (OBJ, XYZ, E57)
  • 360-degree photography with measurement tools
  • Mobile scanning support for iPhone and Android
  • Matterport Pro3 and Pro2 camera hardware ecosystem
  • Cloud processing with ML-powered spatial intelligence

Pros

  • Industry-leading digital twin quality and accuracy
  • End-to-end platform from scanning to sharing
  • Strong real estate and construction industry integrations
  • Accessible mobile scanning without specialized hardware

Cons

  • Pro camera hardware is expensive ($3,395+ for Pro3)
  • Active space limits on starter plans can be restrictive
  • Cloud-dependent—all processing requires internet connection
  • BIM export quality varies for complex architectural details
