About Brex

Brex is an AI-native financial platform providing corporate cards, expense management, and treasury services for startups and enterprises. Its AI-native Accounting API automates accounting workflows end-to-end with real-time ERP integrations. Brex uses AI agents for intelligent finance operations including automated receipt matching, policy enforcement, and accounting automation. Acquired by Capital One in 2026 for $5.15 billion.

Key Features

  • Corporate cards with rewards

  • Policy enforcement AI
  • Treasury and cash management

  • Travel management
