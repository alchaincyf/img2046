About AfterShip

AfterShip is a leading post-purchase platform providing shipment tracking, returns management, and warranty solutions for ecommerce businesses. Its AI features include estimated delivery date predictions that reduce customer anxiety and WISMO (where is my order) inquiries. AfterShip integrates with 1,000+ carriers globally and 50+ ecommerce platforms. The AfterShip Returns Center lets merchants offer branded return experiences while reducing manual work.

Key Features

  • Tracking for 1,100+ carriers globally
  • AI-powered estimated delivery date predictions
  • Branded tracking page with product recommendations
  • Automated WISMO chatbot to reduce support tickets
  • Returns management with customer self-service portal
  • Integrations with Shopify, WooCommerce, BigCommerce, Amazon

Pros

  • Widest carrier coverage globally
  • Reduces WISMO support tickets significantly
  • Easy setup with branded tracking experience
  • Free plan available for small stores

Cons

  • Advanced features gated behind expensive plans
  • Returns module is separate product
  • Some carrier integrations have delays
  • Analytics could be more detailed
