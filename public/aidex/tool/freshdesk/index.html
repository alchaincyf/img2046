About Freshdesk

Freshdesk by Freshworks is used by over 73,000 companies, with its Freddy AI assistant handling about 80% of routine tasks like order tracking and password resets. The platform offers a generous free plan for up to 10 agents, making it accessible for small teams.

Key Features

  • Freddy AI assistant
  • Ticket management
  • Knowledge base
  • Omnichannel support
  • SLA management
  • Automations
  • Marketplace

Pros
