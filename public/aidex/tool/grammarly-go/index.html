About Grammarly GO

Grammarly GO is Grammarly's generative AI writing feature that goes beyond grammar checking to help with writing and rewriting text from scratch. Integrated into the Grammarly ecosystem, it helps users ideate, draft, and revise content in context. It leverages Grammarly's vast understanding of writing quality and style.
