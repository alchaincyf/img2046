About Trainual

Trainual is a business documentation and training platform focused on building company playbooks and onboarding programs. Teams document processes, roles, and policies in structured formats, then assign training to employees with tests and completion tracking. AI features help generate content from prompts and suggest improvements to existing documentation.

Key Features

  • Business playbook creation
  • Employee onboarding workflows
  • Process and policy documentation
  • Testing and knowledge checks
  • Progress tracking and reporting
  • AI content generation
  • Video and multimedia embedding
  • Role-based training assignments
