About Hemingway Editor Plus

Hemingway Editor helps you make writing clear and concise with readability highlights, grammar checking, and instant feedback on passive voice and adverbs. The classic free version color-codes writing problems: yellow (complex), red (very hard), blue (passive), green (adverbs). Hemingway Editor Plus adds AI-powered features including proofreading, tone and style changes, and paraphrasing tools. With one click, you can ask Hemingway to simplify wordy sentences or adjust tone—making your writing more persuasive, casual, or formal. The AI writing assistant suggests synonym options, fixes tricky grammar, and rewrites sentences while keeping your voice. Ideal for writers focused on clarity and readability.

Key Features

  • Color-coded readability highlights
  • U.S. grade level readability scoring
  • Passive voice detection
  • Adverb flagging
