About Google Dialogflow

Google Dialogflow is Google Cloud's comprehensive conversational AI platform for building chatbots and voice agents with advanced natural language understanding. The platform supports 30+ languages and dialects, making it ideal for global applications. Dialogflow offers two editions: Dialogflow ES (Essentials) for basic chatbots and Dialogflow CX for enterprise-scale applications with complex conversation flows. The platform leverages Google's powerful NLU technology, the same used in Google Assistant, to accurately understand user intent across languages and contexts. Dialogflow integrates seamlessly with Google Cloud services and supports deployment across multiple channels including web, mobile, messaging platforms, and phone systems. The platform includes visual flow builders, testing tools, and analytics dashboards.
