About Wonder Studio

Wonder Studio by Wonder Dynamics (acquired by Autodesk) automatically replaces actors with CG characters in live-action footage. Upload your video and 3D character, and the AI handles motion capture, body tracking, lighting estimation, and compositing. It automates 80-90% of the VFX work needed for CG character integration, making high-quality visual effects accessible to independent filmmakers and small studios.

Key Features

  • Automatic CG character replacement
  • AI motion capture from video
  • Body tracking and animation
  • Lighting estimation
  • Automatic compositing
  • Multiple character support
  • Export to Maya, Blender, Unreal
  • Cloud-based processing

Pros

  • Automates 80-90% of VFX work
  • No motion capture suit needed
  • Accessible for indie filmmakers
  • Export to professional tools
  • Free tier available

Cons

  • Complex scenes can fail
  • Processing time for long footage
  • Character rigging must match template
