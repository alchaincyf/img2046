About Gemini for Google Slides

Gemini for Google Slides brings AI capabilities into Google's presentation tool, allowing users to generate slides from prompts, create custom images with Imagen, and get design suggestions. As part of Google Workspace, it integrates with Docs, Sheets, and Gmail for content sourcing. For organizations using Google Workspace, it provides a seamless AI presentation experience within the familiar Google Slides interface.

Key Features

  • AI slide generation from prompts
  • Custom image creation with Imagen
  • Help me organize feature
  • Smart layout suggestions
  • Integration with Google Workspace
  • Real-time collaboration
  • Version history and comments
  • Export to PowerPoint and PDF

Pros

  • Native Google Slides integration
  • Strong real-time collaboration
  • AI image generation built in
  • Leverages Google Workspace content
  • Free Google Slides base product

Cons

  • Gemini features require paid subscription
  • AI design quality behind dedicated tools
  • Limited template variety vs Canva
  • Presentation generation still maturing
