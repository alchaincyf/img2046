About HiredScore

HiredScore specializes in intelligent AI resume screening and talent matching, plugging into existing ATS and HCM systems to automatically score and rank applicants. Its AI reduces bias in screening while dramatically speeding up the initial candidate review process.

Key Features

  • AI resume scoring
  • Talent matching
  • Bias reduction algorithms
  • ATS/HCM integration
  • Passive candidate rediscovery
  • Compliance tools
  • Recruiter efficiency analytics
