About Spellbook

Spellbook is an AI-powered legal drafting tool that works directly in Microsoft Word, where lawyers already draft contracts. It suggests language, flags issues, and helps draft clauses based on context. Spellbook is trained specifically on legal language and contracts, understanding legal concepts and standard terms. It's designed to augment lawyers' drafting workflow rather than replace it.

Key Features

  • AI clause suggestions in Word
  • Contract issue flagging
  • Legal language generation
  • Context-aware drafting assistance
  • Standard term recognition
  • Risk identification
  • Cross-reference checking
  • Clause library integration

Pros

  • Works in Microsoft Word natively
  • Trained specifically on legal language
  • Augments existing workflow
  • Fast clause generation
  • Accessible pricing for solo practitioners

Cons

  • Limited to contract drafting
  • Requires Microsoft Word
  • Less comprehensive than full CLM tools
  • Still requires legal review of all suggestions
