About Motion

Motion is the strongest AI task manager for calendar-driven work, analyzing deadlines, estimated effort, meeting density, and urgency—then continuously rebuilding your schedule in real time. It has evolved into an 'AI Employee SuperApp' that automatically schedules tasks, manages projects, and deploys specialized AI agents for sales, support, marketing, and project management. Motion's intelligent scheduling engine takes into account your existing meetings, task priorities, deadlines, and work patterns to create an optimized daily schedule that adapts dynamically as new tasks and meetings are added. The platform combines calendar management, task tracking, project planning, and meeting scheduling into one unified interface.
