About Meltwater

Meltwater is an enterprise media intelligence platform that combines social listening, media monitoring, PR analytics, and influencer management. It monitors traditional media (TV, radio, print) alongside social media and online sources. Meltwater's AI analyzes sentiment, identifies trends, and measures the impact of PR and social campaigns.

Key Features

  • Social listening
  • Media monitoring (TV/radio/print)
  • Influencer management
  • PR analytics
  • Sentiment analysis
  • Social publishing
  • Custom dashboards

Pros

  • Traditional + social monitoring
  • Strong PR features
  • Enterprise-grade

Cons

  • Complex platform
