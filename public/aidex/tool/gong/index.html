About Gong

Gong pioneered the conversation intelligence category, using AI to analyze every sales call, meeting, and email to surface insights that help teams close more deals. The platform records, transcribes, and analyzes customer interactions to identify winning behaviors, flag risks in active deals, and provide coaching opportunities. Gong's revenue AI agents handle follow-ups automatically, while forecasting features provide unbiased views of pipeline numbers based on actual conversation data rather than rep estimates.

Key Features

  • AI conversation recording and analysis
  • Deal and pipeline analytics
  • Revenue AI agents for follow-ups
  • AI-powered sales forecasting
  • Coaching insights comparing rep performance
  • CRM integration and activity capture
  • Competitive intelligence tracking
  • Custom deal boards and alerts

Pros
