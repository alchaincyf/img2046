About BeyondTrust

BeyondTrust is a leading privileged access management (PAM) platform that secures privileged credentials, controls remote access, and manages endpoint privileges across enterprise environments. The platform comprises Password Safe (credential vaulting and automated rotation), Privileged Remote Access (just-in-time access with session recording), and Endpoint Privilege Management (removing local admin rights while maintaining productivity). BeyondTrust supports Windows, macOS, Linux, and Unix endpoints with centralized policy management.

Key Features

  • Password Safe for privileged credential vaulting and automated rotation
  • Privileged Remote Access with credential injection and session recording
  • Endpoint Privilege Management removing local admin rights
  • Just-in-time privilege elevation for Windows, macOS, and Linux
  • Privileged session monitoring and forensic audit trails
  • Integration with SIEM, ITSM, and identity platforms

Pros

  • Comprehensive PAM suite covering credentials, remote access, and endpoints
  • Strong endpoint privilege management across all major OS
  • Detailed session recording and audit capabilities
  • Leader in Gartner Magic Quadrant for PAM

Cons

  • Products often sold separately, leading to high total costs
  • Pricing is not transparent and requires sales engagement
  • Complex implementation may require professional services
  • Can be cost-prohibitive for smaller organizations
