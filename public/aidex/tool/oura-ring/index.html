About Oura Ring

Oura Ring is a smart wearable ring that tracks sleep, activity, heart rate, body temperature, and blood oxygen levels. Its AI-powered platform delivers daily Readiness, Sleep, and Activity scores along with personalized insights and recommendations. The ring's compact form factor and multi-day battery life make it one of the most convenient health trackers. Oura's AI analyzes trends over time to detect patterns in your health data.

Key Features

  • Sleep stage tracking and analysis
  • Heart rate and HRV monitoring
  • Body temperature sensing
  • Blood oxygen (SpO2) monitoring
  • Daily Readiness Score
  • Activity and workout tracking
  • Cycle tracking for women
  • Multi-day battery life

Pros

  • Excellent sleep tracking accuracy
  • Multi-day battery life
  • Comprehensive health metrics
