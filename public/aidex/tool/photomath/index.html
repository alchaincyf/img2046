About Photomath

Photomath is an AI-powered math learning app that lets students scan handwritten or printed math problems using their phone camera and receive detailed step-by-step solutions. It covers arithmetic through calculus, showing multiple solution methods to help students understand concepts rather than just get answers. Acquired by Google in 2022.

Key Features

  • Camera problem scanning
  • Step-by-step solutions
  • Multiple solution methods
  • Animated explanations
  • Scientific calculator
  • Covers arithmetic to calculus

Pros

  • Camera scanning is fast and accurate
  • Clear step-by-step explanations
  • Multiple solution methods shown
  • Works offline for basic features

Cons

  • Plus subscription needed for detailed explanations
  • Doesn't cover all advanced topics
  • Can encourage answer-copying
