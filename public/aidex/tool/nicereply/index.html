About Nicereply

Nicereply specializes in measuring customer satisfaction after support interactions. It embeds one-click surveys in emails and chat, collecting CSAT, NPS, and CES feedback seamlessly. It integrates with major helpdesk platforms to correlate satisfaction with agent performance.

  • CSAT, NPS, CES metrics
