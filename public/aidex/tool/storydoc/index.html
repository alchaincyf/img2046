About Storydoc

Storydoc creates interactive business presentations designed for B2B sales and marketing. Unlike static slide decks, presentations are web-based with scroll-based stories, embedded videos, interactive charts, and personalization features. The platform tracks every interaction per viewer, showing what slides they viewed, how long they spent, and what they clicked, giving sales teams invaluable intelligence for follow-up.

Key Features

  • Interactive web-based presentations
  • Recipient engagement analytics
  • Slide-by-slide view tracking
  • Personalization tokens
  • CRM integration (Salesforce, HubSpot)
  • Video embedding
  • AI content generation
  • White-labeling and custom domain

Pros
