About Pentera

Pentera is an automated penetration testing and security validation platform that continuously tests your entire attack surface by safely emulating real-world attacks. It discovers vulnerabilities, misconfigurations, and credential exposures across internal networks, external-facing assets, and cloud environments (AWS, Azure). The platform runs non-destructive attack emulations in production without agents, providing validated, risk-prioritized remediation guidance. Pentera integrates with CI/CD pipelines and ITSM tools for automated remediation workflows.

Key Features

  • Automated penetration testing across internal, external, and cloud environments
  • Real-world attack emulation without agents or disruption
  • AI-driven attack path identification and risk prioritization
  • Continuous security validation aligned with CTEM frameworks
  • Compliance testing for PCI DSS, GDPR, HIPAA
  • CI/CD pipeline and ITSM integration for automated remediation

Pros

  • Runs safely in production without disrupting business operations
  • Covers hybrid, cloud-native, and on-premises environments
  • Provides validated, actionable remediation guidance with risk prioritization
  • AI-driven capabilities for complex attack path discovery

Cons

  • High cost starting at ~$35,000/year, not suitable for small businesses
  • Pricing structure can be complex and opaque
  • May require security expertise to fully interpret results
