About Figma

Figma is the world's leading collaborative design platform used by millions of designers and developers. It combines powerful design, prototyping, and developer handoff tools in a single browser-based application. Figma's real-time collaboration features allow entire teams to work together seamlessly, with changes visible instantly to all participants.

Figma includes native AI features that generate designs inside your existing workflow, respecting your design system tokens and components. The platform supports everything from initial wireframes to high-fidelity prototypes with Smart Animate and interactive overlays. All paid seats include access to FigJam for collaborative brainstorming and Figma Slides for presentations.

With unlimited free viewer seats and robust inspection tools for developers, Figma eliminates traditional design-to-development handoff friction. The platform's extensive plugin ecosystem and community resources make it the go-to choice for modern product teams.

Key Features

  • Real-time collaboration with unlimited viewers
  • Native AI design generation within your design system
  • Advanced prototyping with Smart Animate
  • FigJam and Figma Slides included with paid seats
  • Developer handoff with code inspection
  • Design systems and component libraries
  • Version history and branching
  • Extensive plugin ecosystem
  • Auto-layout and constraints
  • Cross-platform (web, desktop apps)
