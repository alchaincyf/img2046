About Flinto

Flinto is a macOS application for creating highly animated and polished app prototypes. Its unique Gesture Designer lets you create custom animations and transitions between screens with precise control over timing and easing. Flinto imports from Sketch and Figma, allowing designers to bring their existing designs to life with sophisticated motion. It's favored by designers who want to showcase app interactions in detail.

Key Features

  • Gesture Designer for custom transition animations
  • Precise control over timing and easing curves
  • Import from Sketch and Figma
  • Scroll behavior and video prototyping
  • Smart components for reusable interactions
  • Mirror for testing on real iOS devices

Pros

  • Best animation control for prototypes
  • One-time purchase, no subscription
  • Great for showcasing app motion design
  • Excellent Sketch integration

Cons

  • Mac only application
  • Less full-featured than Figma for overall design
  • Smaller community
  • Less relevant as Figma adds animation features
