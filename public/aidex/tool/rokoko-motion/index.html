About Rokoko

Rokoko is a motion capture hardware and software company making professional mocap accessible to indie developers, animation studios, and virtual production. The Rokoko Smartsuit Pro is a full-body mocap suit with 19 wireless sensors capturing at 100fps, while the Smartgloves add finger tracking. Rokoko Studio software provides real-time mocap streaming, retargeting to custom rigs, and AI-powered animation cleanup. The Rokoko Video tool uses computer vision AI to convert regular video footage into 3D animations without any mocap hardware, making it a popular choice for content creators.

Key Features

  • Rokoko Video: AI video-to-3D motion extraction from any camera
  • Smartsuit Pro: Full-body wireless mocap at 100fps
  • Smartgloves: 12-sensor finger tracking accessory
  • Real-time mocap streaming to Unity, Unreal Engine, iClone, Maya
  • AI-powered animation cleanup and smoothing
  • Rig retargeting for custom character animations
  • Rokoko Studio: mocap management and editing software
  • BVH, FBX, C3D export for all major 3D applications

Pros

  • Most affordable full-body mocap hardware on market
  • Rokoko Video enables animation from any video source
  • Real-time streaming directly into game engines is powerful for virtual production
  • Good community and plugin support for all major platforms

Cons

  • Video-based AI animation quality lower than hardware mocap
  • Inertial sensors drift over long sessions without magnetic corrections
  • Hardware upfront cost still significant for hobbyists
  • Finger tracking requires Smartgloves add-on (additional cost)
