About Scaniverse

Scaniverse by Niantic (makers of Pokemon Go) is a free 3D scanning app for iOS devices. It supports both LiDAR scanning and photogrammetry modes, creating detailed 3D models of objects, rooms, and outdoor spaces. The app processes scans locally on-device for privacy, and supports Gaussian splatting for photorealistic captures. Models can be shared, exported in multiple formats, or viewed in AR.

Key Features

  • LiDAR and photogrammetry scanning
  • On-device processing
  • Gaussian splatting support
  • AR viewing
  • Multiple export formats
  • Social sharing features
  • Object and room scanning
  • No cloud processing needed

Pros

  • Completely free
  • On-device processing for privacy
  • Gaussian splatting for quality
  • Made by Niantic (Pokemon Go)
  • No subscription needed

Cons

  • iOS only
  • Best results require LiDAR device
  • Limited export formats vs Polycam
