About Blooma

Blooma uses AI to automate commercial real estate underwriting, helping lenders and investors evaluate deals faster and more accurately. The platform collects and analyzes data from property records, borrower history, market comparables, and economic indicators to create comprehensive risk profiles and financial models. What traditionally takes days of manual analysis, Blooma compresses into minutes, enabling faster deal decisions with greater confidence.
