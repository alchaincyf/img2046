About GoSearch

GoSearch is an AI-powered enterprise search platform that stands out for its transparent, scalable pricing and fast deployment. Using a hybrid indexing approach combining semantic and lexical search, it provides comprehensive workplace search without the complexity and cost of competitors like Glean. Every plan includes AI-powered semantic search, native data connectors, and built-in analytics—features that other platforms often charge extra for. GoSearch positions itself as the practical choice for organizations that need enterprise search without enterprise-grade pricing.

Key Features

  • Hybrid semantic and lexical search
  • Native data connectors for major apps
  • AI-powered answer generation
  • Built-in analytics dashboard
  • Permission-aware results
  • Fast deployment in hours
  • Knowledge base management
  • Flexible LLM and RAG architecture

Pros
