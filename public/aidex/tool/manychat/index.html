About ManyChat

ManyChat is the leading chat marketing platform for automating conversations on Instagram DMs, Facebook Messenger, WhatsApp, and Telegram. It enables automated responses to comments, story mentions, and keywords, driving engagement and sales. ManyChat's visual flow builder makes creating chatbot sequences easy without coding.

Key Features

  • Facebook Messenger bots
  • WhatsApp automation
  • Visual flow builder
  • Comment automation
  • Story mention replies
  • Growth tools
