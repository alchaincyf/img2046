About BILL

BILL (formerly Bill.com) automates accounts payable and accounts receivable processes for mid-market businesses. Its AI extracts invoice data, routes approvals, detects anomalies, and manages vendor payments. BILL processes billions of dollars in payments annually and integrates with major accounting systems including QuickBooks, Xero, and NetSuite.

Key Features

  • AI invoice data extraction
  • Automated approval routing
  • ACH, check, and card payments
  • Vendor management
  • AR invoicing and collections
  • Anomaly detection
  • Accounting system sync
  • Audit trail

Pros

  • Comprehensive AP and AR automation
  • Strong accounting integrations
  • Reliable payment processing
  • Good approval workflows
  • Trusted by mid-market companies

Cons

  • Per-user pricing can be expensive
  • Interface can feel dated
  • Setup takes time
  • Transaction fees on some payment types
