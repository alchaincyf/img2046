About LALAL.AI

LALAL.AI is a leading AI-powered audio separation tool that can extract 8 different stems from any audio: vocals, instrumental, drums, bass, piano, synthesizer, acoustic guitar, and electric guitar. Powered by the proprietary Perseus neural network based on transformer models, it delivers clean separations with nuanced harmonics and tonal qualities.

Key Features

  • 8-stem audio separation
  • Vocal removal
  • Voice Cleaner for noise removal
  • Video audio processing
  • Batch processing
  • High-quality Perseus neural network
