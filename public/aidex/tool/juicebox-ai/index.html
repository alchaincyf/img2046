About Juicebox AI (PeopleGPT)

Juicebox AI, known for its PeopleGPT product, lets recruiters find candidates using plain English descriptions instead of complex Boolean strings. Its AI interprets natural language queries and searches across a vast talent database to surface highly qualified matches.

Key Features

  • Natural language candidate search
  • AI-powered matching
  • Contact information
  • Chrome extension
  • Candidate list export
  • Email outreach
  • Boolean + AI search

Pros

  • Natural language queries are intuitive
  • Free plan available
  • Fast and accurate results
  • No Boolean expertise needed

Cons

  • Database not as large as LinkedIn
  • Limited CRM features
  • Contact info accuracy varies
