About arXiv

arXiv is an open-access preprint repository hosting over 2.4 million scholarly articles in physics, mathematics, computer science, and more. Founded in 1991, it has fundamentally changed how research is communicated in the physical and computational sciences.

Key Features

  • Open-access preprint hosting
  • 2.4M+ scholarly articles
  • RSS feeds
  • LaTeX source availability
  • API for developers

Pros

  • Completely free and open access
  • Instant publication
  • Cornerstone of modern research
  • Strong community

Cons

  • No peer review
  • Quality varies
  • Basic search
  • Limited to certain disciplines
