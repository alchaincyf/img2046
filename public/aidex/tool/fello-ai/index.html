About Fello

Fello is a unique AI tool that integrates with existing real estate CRMs to identify, score, and convert leads most likely to sell their homes within the next six months. The platform analyzes millions of data points from each contact in an agent's database, scoring them on sell probability. Fello then powers hyper-personalized AI marketing campaigns targeting these high-probability sellers, helping agents convert their existing contacts into active listings rather than just chasing new leads.

Key Features

  • AI seller probability scoring
  • CRM database analysis
  • Hyper-personalized marketing campaigns
  • 6-month sell prediction
  • Lead priority ranking
  • Automated outreach
  • Home value widget
  • Market report generation

Pros

  • Converts existing database into seller leads
  • Strong predictive accuracy
  • Integrates with existing CRM
  • Focus on sellers (underserved niche)

Cons

  • Custom pricing not transparent
