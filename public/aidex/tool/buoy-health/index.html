About Buoy Health

Buoy Health uses AI to help people understand their symptoms and navigate to the most appropriate healthcare resources. The platform asks dynamic questions based on clinical algorithms, then provides personalized recommendations for care options including telehealth, urgent care, or self-care. Buoy partners with health systems, insurers, and employers to improve healthcare navigation and reduce unnecessary ER visits.

Key Features

  • AI symptom assessment and triage
  • Personalized care navigation
  • Provider and resource matching
  • Dynamic clinical questioning
  • Integration with health system directories
  • Employee wellness integration

Pros

  • Free and accessible for consumers
  • Helps reduce unnecessary ER visits
  • Connects users to appropriate care resources
  • Strong health system partnerships

Cons

  • Limited availability outside the US
  • Care recommendations depend on local partnerships
  • Less clinical depth than some competitors
