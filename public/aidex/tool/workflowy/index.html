About WorkFlowy

WorkFlowy is a clean and distraction-free app designed for capturing thoughts, managing tasks, and outlining complex ideas effectively. The platform features infinitely nested lists that work offline and automatically sync across desktop and web versions. WorkFlowy's outline method organizes notes in a hierarchical structure, with key ideas at the top level and details nested beneath. A standout feature is Live Copies that allow you to duplicate text that updates in real-time everywhere it appears, enhancing consistency across your notes. In 2026, version 2026.02 introduced Calendar/Today integration, Numbered Lists, Code and Quote Blocks, and cross-tab drag and drop in Chrome, Edge, Firefox, and Safari on Mac.
