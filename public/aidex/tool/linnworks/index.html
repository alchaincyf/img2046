About Linnworks

Linnworks is a multi-channel inventory management system that processes over $15 billion in annual GMV and uses AI-powered forecasting tools to predict inventory needs by analyzing location-specific factors and historical sales data across more than 100 marketplaces and sales channels. A documented case study showed Graff City reduced inventory management time by 87.5% (from 8 hours to 30 minutes per week) while completely eliminating stockouts. Linnworks covers order management, warehouse operations, shipping, and listing management from one platform.

Key Features

  • AI demand forecasting across location-specific sales patterns
  • 100+ marketplace and sales channel integrations
  • Centralized order management with automated routing
  • Warehouse management with pick-pack-ship workflows
  • Multi-carrier shipping management and label printing
  • Listing management and catalog sync across channels

Pros

  • Widest marketplace integration library at 100+ channels
  • Proven at scale processing $15B+ in annual GMV
  • Free tier available for small operations to evaluate the platform
  • Comprehensive order, inventory, and warehouse tools in one system

Cons

  • Interface can feel dated compared to newer SaaS alternatives
  • Paid plans jump significantly from free — limited mid-tier options
  • Setup and configuration requires time and technical familiarity
  • Customer support quality inconsistent during peak periods
