About Looka

Looka (founded 2016 as Logojoy) is a comprehensive AI-driven branding platform that goes beyond logo generation to provide complete brand identity solutions. It leverages AI to generate logos tailored to user preferences and includes business cards, social media branding kits, email signatures, and other branding assets. Looka combines both design and commerce, making it easy for entrepreneurs and small businesses to create professional branding quickly and effortlessly. The AI handles color schemes, fonts, and layouts to ensure cohesive brand identity.
