About Unity AI

Unity AI provides integrated AI tools within the Unity game engine, including ML-Agents for training intelligent NPC behavior through reinforcement learning, Sentis for running neural networks in runtime, and Unity Muse for AI-assisted content creation. ML-Agents lets NPCs learn from player behavior, adapt difficulty dynamically, and evolve strategies. Unity remains the cornerstone of indie and mid-size game development with its extensive Asset Store and cross-platform deployment.
