About Perplexity AI

Perplexity AI is a leading AI search engine that delivers clear, citation-backed answers in a conversational format, combining real-time web browsing with AI models like its proprietary Sonar model, GPT-5, and Claude 4 Sonnet. It has become one of the most popular alternatives to traditional search engines, known for its ability to synthesize information from multiple sources and present it with transparent citations. Perplexity excels at deep research tasks, offering multi-step reasoning in its Pro Search mode that follows up on initial queries to build comprehensive answers. The platform serves millions of users who want accurate, sourced answers rather than lists of links.

Key Features

  • Citation-backed conversational answers
  • Pro Search with multi-step reasoning
  • Access to GPT-5, Claude 4, and Sonar models
  • Real-time web browsing and data synthesis
  • File upload and document analysis
  • Collections for organizing research
  • AI image generation
  • Perplexity Pages for publishing
