About PhotoRoom

PhotoRoom is an AI-powered photo editor that creates professional-looking images for social media and e-commerce. Its AI removes backgrounds instantly, generates new backgrounds, and creates batch-edited product photos. The tool is particularly popular with social media managers and e-commerce sellers who need polished visuals quickly.

Key Features

  • AI background removal
  • AI background generation
  • Batch editing
  • Product photo templates
  • Text styles
  • Shadow & reflection
  • Export in multiple sizes
