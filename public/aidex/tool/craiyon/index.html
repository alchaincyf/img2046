About Craiyon

Craiyon (formerly DALL-E mini) is the internet's favorite lightweight, browser-based AI image generator that's completely free and requires no login. While many tools have moved behind paywalls, Craiyon maintains its 'Free Forever' promise. It's famous for generating a grid of 9 images at once, giving users multiple options to choose from. In 2026, it features advanced Style Presets (Vector, Photo, Raw) and a built-in background remover. Perfect for casual users, meme creators, and anyone wanting quick AI art without commitment.
