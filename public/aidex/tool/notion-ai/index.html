About Notion AI

Notion AI brings powerful AI writing capabilities directly into the Notion workspace, eliminating the need to switch between tools. It leverages both GPT-4.1 and Claude 3.7 Sonnet to provide intelligent writing assistance, brainstorming, summarization, and editing. Notion AI understands the context of your workspace, allowing it to reference existing pages and generate content that aligns with your notes and documentation. Now included in Business and Enterprise plans (no separate add-on needed), Notion AI offers exceptional value for teams already using Notion as their knowledge management platform.

Key Features

  • Access to GPT-4.1 and Claude 3.7 Sonnet
  • Context-aware writing assistance
  • Brainstorming and ideation
  • Summarization of pages and documents
  • Automatic action items extraction
  • Tone and length adjustment
  • Translation (100+ languages)
  • Integrated with all Notion features
