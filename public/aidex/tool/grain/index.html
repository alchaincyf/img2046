About Grain

Grain is an AI-powered meeting recorder and note-taker that automatically identifies and highlights key moments from video meetings. It creates shareable video clips with transcripts, making it easy to share insights with team members who missed the meeting.

Key Features

  • AI meeting recording
  • Auto-highlight detection
  • Shareable video clips
  • Searchable transcripts
  • CRM integration
  • Coaching insights
  • Team library

Pros

  • Useful free tier
